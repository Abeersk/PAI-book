# APA Citation Guide for Module 2

## Purpose
This guide provides the standard format for citing sources in Module 2 chapters following APA 7th edition style.

## General Format for In-Text Citations
- (Author, Year) for paraphrased information
- (Author, Year, p. #) for direct quotes
- Multiple authors: (Author et al., Year) for 3+ authors after first citation

## Common Source Types

### Books
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work*. Publisher.

Example: Corke, P. (2017). *Robotics, vision and control: Fundamental algorithms in MATLAB* (2nd ed.). Springer.

### Journal Articles
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

Example: Koenig, N., & Howard, A. (2004). Design and use paradigms for Gazebo, an open-source multi-robot simulator. *IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems*, 3, 2149-2154.

### Web Pages
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Date). Title of webpage. *Website Name*. URL

Example: Open Robotics. (2023, June 15). Gazebo robotics simulator. *Gazebo*. https://gazebosim.org/

### Conference Papers
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of the Conference Name* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher. URL

## Specific Robotics Sources

### ROS Documentation
ROS.org. (Year). *ROS 2 documentation*. https://docs.ros.org/en/rolling/

### Gazebo Documentation
Open Robotics. (Year). *Gazebo documentation*. https://gazebosim.org/docs

### Unity Robotics
Unity Technologies. (Year). *Unity robotics packages*. https://docs.unity3d.com/Packages/com.unity.robotics@latest

## Citation Examples in Text

### Paraphrasing
Digital twins enable safe robot development by providing virtual environments for testing (Rasheed et al., 2020).

### Direct Quote
According to Koenig and Howard (2004), "Gazebo provides a robust physics engine for accurate robot simulation" (p. 2150).

### Multiple Sources
Physics simulation in robotics has been extensively studied (Koenig & Howard, 2004; Corke, 2017; Rasheed et al., 2020).

## Reference List Format
Create a section at the end of each chapter titled "References" with full citations in alphabetical order by author's last name.

## Quality Control
- Verify all in-text citations have corresponding references
- Check that all references are accessible and current
- Ensure citations support factual claims made in content
- Use authoritative sources (official documentation, peer-reviewed articles)
